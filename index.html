FAQ

  • Does OceanPlay include channels or content?
    No. OceanPlay is a media player and does not provide any content or playlists. Users must provide their own playlists.
  • My playlist doesn’t load.
    Verify your server, username and password. If the issue persists, contact support with a screenshot and your tvOS version.
  • Playback issues or buffering.
    Check your internet connection and try a different stream. Some providers may throttle or have unstable servers.
